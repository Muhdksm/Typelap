About Typelap

Typelap is an online typing practice platform designed to help you type faster, smarter, and more accurately. Track your progress, improve your WPM, and challenge yourself daily.

Our mission is to provide a fun and effective way to improve typing skills for everyone — from beginners to advanced users.

Created by Muhammad Kasim, Typelap combines speed, accuracy, and gamification to make typing practice enjoyable and productive.
